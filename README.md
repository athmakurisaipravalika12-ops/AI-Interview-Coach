# AI Interview Coach

## Project Overview

AI Interview Coach is an AI-powered interview preparation platform designed to help students, fresh graduates, and job seekers improve their interview skills through realistic mock interviews and personalized feedback.

The platform creates a virtual interview environment where users can select a topic such as Python, SQL, Flask, or general aptitude and answer interview questions. The AI system evaluates the responses, provides constructive feedback, and identifies areas that need improvement.

The main goal of the project is to make interview preparation more effective, accessible, and personalized for every learner.

## Problem Statement

Many students struggle during job interviews because they do not receive enough practice opportunities or personalized guidance. Traditional preparation methods often involve reading notes and watching videos, but they do not provide real-time evaluation of answers.

As a result, students may:

* Lack confidence during interviews
* Be unaware of their weak areas
* Have difficulty improving communication skills
* Be unprepared for technical and behavioral questions

## Proposed Solution

AI Interview Coach solves this problem by providing an interactive platform where users can practice interviews anytime. The system asks questions, analyzes answers, and generates feedback that helps users improve their performance.

The platform acts like a virtual interviewer and mentor, helping users gain confidence before attending actual interviews.

## Key Features

* User Registration and Login
* Topic-Based Interview Preparation
* Mock Interview Sessions
* AI-Powered Answer Evaluation
* Performance Scoring System
* Personalized Feedback
* Progress Tracking
* Interview History Storage
* Skill Improvement Recommendations

## How the System Works

1. User logs into the platform.
2. User selects an interview topic.
3. The system generates relevant interview questions.
4. User submits answers.
5. AI evaluates the responses.
6. Performance score is calculated.
7. Feedback and improvement suggestions are displayed.
8. Results are stored for future reference.
